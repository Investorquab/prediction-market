# ⬡ GenMarket — AI-Verified Prediction Market

Decentralized prediction market powered by GenLayer intelligent contracts. Anyone creates a market, anyone bets YES or NO, and when the deadline passes, GenLayer AI validators independently verify the real-world outcome and reach consensus — no human judges, no oracles, no trust required.
